Activation KPIs and tracking logic
Toolkit adoption

KPI: 3-5 European distributors must demonstrably use the developed marketing toolkit materials during the first activation period.

Distributor activation

KPI: these distributors complete at least 2 marketing activities, such as social media campaigns, online promotion or co-advertising. The portal tracks this through completed calendar items, used assets and activity logs.

Usability feedback

KPI: at least 60% of participating distributors rate the developed materials as usable and applicable, tracked through feedback forms, interviews and admin review.

Digital performance proof

Supporting signal: track impressions, reach, engagement rate, clicks, saves, shares, watch time, downloads, inquiries and qualitative B2B reactions using distributor screenshots, platform exports and portal usage logs.

Sales follow-up signal

Not a core KPI. Use as long-term context: compare quote requests, distributor inquiries and monthly YDG/YDP sales with a pre-campaign baseline where data is available.

Minimum rhythm

2 product posts per month per active product line, plus one sales follow-up using a brochure, datasheet or comparison sheet.

Use when

Distributor has limited marketing capacity and sales or management handles communication.

RHY

Standard rhythm

1 activation per week during campaign periods: one social post, one website or email action, and one sales follow-up moment.

Use when

1-2 posts per week, monthly YouTube or application content, local campaign examples and contribution of market assets back to Yanmar.

Use when

Distributor has stronger digital maturity and can support coordinated marketing activity, co-advertising or performance reporting.

Status logic: Not started means no portal activity yet. On schedule means early activity is started. On track means at least half of the expected activation signals are visible.
